Communication

Communication between the instructional team and students is paramount for a successful learning environment. Students are strongly encouraged, at their convenience, to reach out to members of the instructional team with any questions.

Slack will serve as the primary tool for communication between students and the instructional team. Before the first class, you will receive an invitation to our Slack room:

http://fewd82.slack.com

Slack can be accessed through the browser, or, through a native application that is installed and runs on your machine.

Homework

Homework will be assigned at the end of every Wednesday and is due the following Monday. Files and materials for homework will be distributed during class, usually during lab, through Slack. Students will push, or upload, their completed homework to their respective Github repositories for review and grading.

Graduation Requirements

In order to successfully complete FEWD, students must:

Homework

Complete 80% of assignments

Attendance

Miss no more than two classes

Final Project

Turn in a project highlighting key course concepts
